Our Story

TringMe provides a unified platform offering,an offering that integrates all the existing voice and telephony communication devices and opens up new avenues to innovate such a platform. TringMe's uniqueness is in it's ability to make and receive call directly from the web - no software to download or install,it's ability to unify voice across traditional telephony options(landlines,cellphones)and the internet.

TringMe also provides a complete ecosystem wherein VOIP providers,Service providers,Enterprise,Developers and End-users can play.An ecosystem which can cater to developing innovative applications that integrate Voice and Telephony without worrying about the underlying details of call routing and signalling.

Innovations

TringMe became the pioneer with its pathbreaking pure web-based calling technology in 2007 followed by the first-ever Mobile VoIP app for Blackberry--a feat deemed impossible at that time. TringMe then brought innovation into the enterprise IVR market by creating VoicePHP solution which simplified developing interactive voice apps (IVR etc.) immensely and was recognized as a viable alternative to VoiceXML. Since then, service providers and companies across the world have deployed TringMe's technology to offer extraordinary services in tele-medicine, entertainment, hospitality, VoIP reselling and others. Little wonder that TringMe was awarded the most innovative startup of India by none other than NASSCOM.
